📊 How to Use Team Finance

Simple step-by-step guide to analyze your business finances and get professional insights in minutes.

1

Getting Started

What is Team Finance? It's a simple tool that takes your financial documents and gives you professional business insights - like having a finance expert on your team!

What you need:

  • Your business financial documents (balance sheet, income statement, bank statements)
  • 5-10 minutes of your time
  • No finance knowledge required!

Dashboard main page showing navigation menu

2

Upload Your Documents

How to upload:

  • Click "Upload Files" in the top menu
  • Drag and drop your files OR click "Choose Files"
  • Wait 5-10 seconds for processing
  • Done! Your files are uploaded

What files can you upload? PDF, Excel, CSV, Word documents

File upload page with drag & drop area

Quick Tip

Upload clear, readable documents for best results. Bank statements and balance sheets work great!

3

Create Your Balance Sheet

Easy steps:

  • Go to Tools → Balance Sheet Generator
  • Select your uploaded files
  • Click "Generate Balance Sheet"
  • Review the results
  • Download your professional balance sheet

Balance Sheet Generator page with file selection

What happens? The system automatically finds your assets, liabilities, and equity from your documents and creates a professional balance sheet.

4

Create Cash Flow Statement

Two options:

  • Option 1: Upload existing cash flow statement
  • Option 2: Auto-generate from your balance sheet

For auto-generation:

  • Go to Tools → Cash Flow Generator
  • Choose "Generate from Balance Sheet"
  • Wait for processing (30-60 seconds)
  • Download your cash flow statement

Cash Flow Generator page showing options

5

See Your Business Insights

Where to look:

  • Click "Analytics" in the top menu
  • View your Financial Health Score (0-100)
  • Check the Risk Level (Low/Medium/High)
  • Read AI recommendations

What you'll see:

  • Health Score: Overall financial health rating
  • Risk Assessment: Chances of financial problems
  • Recommendations: What to do to improve
  • Trends: How your business is performing over time

Analytics dashboard showing health score and charts

Radar chart showing financial metrics

6

Compare with Big Companies

How to search:

  • Click "Search" in the top menu
  • Type any company name (Apple, Google, Microsoft)
  • See their financial analysis
  • Compare with your business

Search model with company results

Smart Tip

Compare your business with companies in the same industry for better insights!

❓

Common Questions

File won't upload?

  • Check file size (must be under 25MB)
  • Make sure it's PDF, Excel, CSV or Word format
  • Try refreshing the page

Results don't look right?

  • Make sure your documents have clear financial tables
  • Try uploading additional supporting documents
  • Check that numbers in your document are readable

How accurate are the predictions?

  • 70-85% accurate with good quality documents
  • Better accuracy with 2-3 years of data
  • Use as guidance, not absolute truth
